Common Questions

Before you apply.

Do I need a technical background?

No. The bootcamp is designed for leaders with zero coding experience. If you can use a laptop and follow instructions, you'll be building by hour two.

What do I need to bring?

A Mac or Linux laptop. We'll walk you through all software setup on Day 1. A pre-bootcamp setup guide is sent one week before.

Is company-confidential code involved?

No. All exercises use sample codebases and open-source projects. For Private Team Bootcamps, we can work with your legal team on a tailored setup.

What happens after the 2 days?

You get 30-day access to a private Slack channel with your cohort and the instructor, plus session recordings for reference. Private teams get 90 days of implementation support.

What if it's not a fit?

If by lunch on Day 1 you don't see the value, we'll issue a full refund. No questions.

Can I send my EA to handle registration?

Yes. Submit the application or have them download the PDF syllabus and handle logistics directly. We'll confirm everything via email.
